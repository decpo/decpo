Assessing Methodological Reliability in the A-Maze Paradigm

Research Area: Psycholinguistics / Sentence Processing

Overview

This project examines the reliability of observing locality effects of long-distance dependencies using the A-maze paradigm. The Maze paradigm has been proposed as an alternative to self-paced reading and eye-tracking paradigms in sentence processing research because it forces an incremental, word-by-word lexical decision process at every step, preventing readers from skipping words, regressing, or employing reading strategies that often obscure real-time processing difficulty in traditional methods.
